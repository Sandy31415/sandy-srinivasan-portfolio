Scented Essentials

This icon set allowed me to explore the challenge of representing scents through purely visual means. Each icon depicts an item with a distinctive, floral scent. Each item was designed in Adobe Illustrator to form a clean and cohesive collection that captures their essence without relying on fragrance itself.
