My Evolution As a Designer

About This Project

This magazine chronicles my first year in GBDA, a pivotal period in my design journey. Working over two months, I photographed locations and landmarks throughout Waterloo. This magazine was created using a combination of Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. It became both a technical exploration and a personal reflection on the experiences that shaped me as a designer.
