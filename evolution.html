My Evolution As a Designer

This magazine was a project I worked on over a fairly long period of time. The graphics were all images I captured around my current city, Waterloo. I used a combination of Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign, which I properly explord for the first time. I used this magazine as a way to reflect on my first year in my program, GBDA. I truly believe that my first year in university was a pivotal time in my journey as a designer, and I'm glad to have had the opportunity to express that.
