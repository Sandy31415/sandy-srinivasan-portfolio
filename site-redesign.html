Reimagining the Aurora Historical Society Website

Project Description

These mockups reimagine the Aurora Historical Society's website to better reflect the breadth of their community work. I felt that the original site didn't capture the scope of their preservation and education work. I designed a modern, welcoming interface that highlights their importance to the community. The redesign features my own photography of Hillary House and the Koffler Museum of Medicine, along with custom newsletter covers that give visitors a preview of their content.
