Reimagining the Aurora Historical Society Website

sandysri0711@gmail.com
www.linkedin.com/in/sandy-srinivasan

I created these mockups as part of an assignment to criritque and reimagine a website belonging to a small business or organization. I felt that the Aurora Historical Society's website didn't quite capture all the work that they did for their town; I wanted my redesign to look modern and welcoming, and to highlight the range of work they do, from preservation to education to fun events. I used images of Hillary House and the Koffler Museum of Medicine that I took myself, as well as creating covers for the newsletters to better highlight their content.
