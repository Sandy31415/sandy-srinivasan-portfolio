RecipeNote: A Recipe App

1 / 7

When prompted to explore creating an app that centered around recipes, I chose to target indecisive users- the kind who spend more time searching for recipes than cooking, or who always have ingredients they can never seem to use up. The app features recommendations based on the users' preferences and history, a library where users can store recipes, modes of instruction that cater to both visual and text-based learners, and a scanner that recommends recipes based on the content of the user's pantry or refrigerator.
