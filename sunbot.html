SunBot- Health Management Design

Managing a chronic health condition can be challenging, especially for senior citizens hoping to retain their independence. My team and I designed SunBot, a smart pill dispenser and tracker with a tactile, button focused interface to help elderly users manage their medications and lifestyle adjustments with ease.
